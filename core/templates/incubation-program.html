STARTUP INCUBATION PROGRAM

deadline: 15 january Apply

The Incubation program by Next Step Innovation Center is designed to support start-upsand companies from concept to development of a workable demo (A Minimum Viable Product).

Eligibility

  • Startups and spin-offs that develop their activity, directly or indirectly, within the scope of AgriTech, EdTech,FinTech,GameTech, FemTech, HealthTech, TravelTech, Cultural and Creative Industries, Green Economy,or Information and Communication Technologies, Cloud Computing, SaaS technology, E-Commerce areconsidered eligible for this.
  • Startups have a business mission to be developed. Startups will have to have up to a maximum of 6months of activity to be considered under the incubation program.
  • All candidate projects are analyzed individually, considering the framing of Next Step Innovation Center'sstrategic areas, the capacity of the team to pursue the objectives that the project proposes to develop,and the potential of the idea to be realized.
  • Applicant startup teams will be considered according to how much time team members are able to sparefor the development of the idea.
  • Professional Working Proficiency level of English Language at least founders.
